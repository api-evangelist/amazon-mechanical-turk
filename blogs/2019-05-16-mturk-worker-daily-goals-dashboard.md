<p>We all set goals around increasing our income but this is easier to do with the right information and tools. Workers have told us that they want better visibility into tracking their earnings over the course of the day. MTurk is happy to announce that we have launched a new Daily Goal Tracker designed to help Workers set earning and HIT completion targets and track their daily progress. <br /> <br />This new dashboard enables Workers to set goals on the number of HITs completed as well as their earnings. This tool can be used to give Workers more visibility into their cumulative earnings over the course of the day. For Workers who want to earn a certain amount on MTurk each day, this tool allows them to see progress on the number of HITs and the estimated pending earnings from those HITs in real time. Please note this tool only includes completed HITs, not those sitting in your queue and that earning estimates assume that all your completed HITs are approved by the Requester. <br /> <br />You can find this new tool on the Dashboard tab on the <a href="https://worker.mturk.com/">Worker MTurk site</a>.</p><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/629/1*M84_ahHf65hhHGtgqX7Mfw.png" /></figure><p>In combination with the Worker Dashboard, this new Daily Goals Dashboard will help Workers meet their daily, weekly and monthly MTurk HIT completion, and earning targets by providing real time feedback on their progress against goals.</p><img alt="" height="1" src="https://medium.com/_/stat?event=post.clientViewed&amp;referrerSource=full_rss&amp;postId=9a18c25a2d7e" width="1" /><hr /><p><a href="https://blog.mturk.com/mturk-worker-daily-goals-dashboard-9a18c25a2d7e">MTurk Worker Daily Goals Dashboard</a> was originally published in <a href="https://blog.mturk.com">Happenings at MTurk</a> on Medium, where people are continuing the conversation by highlighting and responding to this story.</p>
